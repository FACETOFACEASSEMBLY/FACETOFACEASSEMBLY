About Face to Face Assembly

Learn more about our mission, vision, and the people behind the work of God in Sapele.

Face to Face Assembly, Sapele, is a Christian ministry devoted to transforming lives through faith, teachiing the word of God, prayer, education and compassion. Our work spans outreach programs, orphanage support, free education for children, and building a strong spiritual community of praying christians.

Our Founder

Pastor Happy Overah

General Overseer of Face to Face Assembly, Pastor Overah has been committed to serving God faithfully for decades. His vision is to build a ministry that brings hope to the less privileged and strengthens the faith of believers through teaching and outreach.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To preach the Gospel of Jesus Christ and extend God’s love through charitable works, free education, and outreach programs.

Vision

To see a community where faith thrives and no child is denied the opportunity of education or care due to poverty.

Our Journey

2005

Face to Face Assembly was founded with the aim of spreading the Gospel and supporting the Sapele community.

2012

Jesus The Son of God Educational Center was established as a free school for children from disadvantaged homes.

2019

We expanded our orphanage support program to reach multiple homes in Delta State.
