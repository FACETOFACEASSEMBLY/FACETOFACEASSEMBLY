About Face to Face Assembly

Face to Face Assembly (also known as Face to Face Christian Assembly) is a church located in Sapele, Delta State. We are on a mission "to usher as many souls as possible into God's Kingdom through the Media, Prayer, Internet, and Mission Trips." Founded to serve the community, we emphasise charity and reaching out to the poor.

General Overseer

Pastor Happy Overah

General Overseer — Pastor Happy Overah leads Face to Face Assembly with a deep commitment to charity, community service and evangelism. Under his leadership the church has initiated projects such as the Jesus The Son of God Educational Center (a free school) and Ebenezer Orphanage Home.

Vision & Mission

We believe Christianity is a communal call to help the helpless. Our mission includes education for poor children, care for motherless homes, and regular outreach that demonstrates God's love in practical ways.
