Our Ministries

Education — Jesus The Son of God Educational Center

A free school established to give poor children access to basic education. Volunteers and donations support teachers, books and uniforms.

Orphanage Support — Ebenezer Orphanage Home

We support Ebenezer Orphanage Home and other motherless homes through feeding programs, supplies and regular visits.

Feeding & Outreach

Regular community feeding initiatives at Testimony Motherless Baby Home and Alfa and Omega Motherless Home, Sapele.

Media & Evangelism

Media-based outreach, prayer meetings and mission trips to spread the gospel and help communities in need.
